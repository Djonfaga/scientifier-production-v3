Core Techniques We Use

These aren't gimmicks. They're research-backed methods with decades of evidence behind them.

⏱️ Spaced Repetition

Review information at optimal intervals, just before you forget. Our AI calculates your personal retention curve.

  • Learn new material
  • Review after 1 day
  • Review after 3 days
  • Review after 7 days, then monthly

🧩 Active Recall

Don't re-read—retrieve. Testing yourself forces the brain to strengthen memory traces.

  • Close your notes
  • Try to recall information from memory
  • Check and correct mistakes
  • Repeat until automatic

🌙 Focused & Diffuse Modes

Your brain has two learning modes. Toggle between focused attention and relaxed thinking for optimal results.

  • Focus intensely for 25-50 minutes
  • Take a break (walk, stretch)
  • Let diffuse mode process in background
  • Return with fresh insights

🔀 Interleaving

Don't practice one skill in isolation. Mix up different topics to build discrimination and transfer.

  • Mix different problem types
  • Vary vocabulary topics within sessions
  • Switch before feeling "done"
  • Build flexible knowledge
